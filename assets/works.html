Colors game

Try out my demo (that may never get its full game), made as my graduation project.

Play as a small girl, whose world is devoid of all color, and has been as long as she can remember. Stepping out of her home she finds herself in a world completly different from what she knew, full of color. This gives her a chance to bring color to her own world.

Game was made with Unity3D and coded in C#. Art was made by me as well, in Krita/Photoshop and Illustrator.
